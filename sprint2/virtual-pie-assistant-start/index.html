You will need the following ingredients:

  • 7 Apples
  • 2 Pie Crusts
  • 0.5 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 egg

Directions:

  1. Place oven rack in the center position and preheat the oven to 400°F
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, light brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice and lemon zest; toss to coat evenly.
  3. Spoon the apple filling over the bottom crust. Roll out the second disc of pie crust until it is ⅛" thick and lay it over the apple filling.
  4. Brush the surface of the pie crust with the egg wash
  5. Cover the edges with a pie shield or a strip of foil to keep them from over browning during the first 25 minutes.
  6. Bake at 400°F (204°C) for 25 minutes.Carefully remove the pie shield, turn the oven down to 375° and continue to bake for an additional 30-35 minutes
